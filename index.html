Privacy Policy for AutoReply Pro

Last updated: November 23, 2025

TL;DR: AutoReply Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Introduction

AutoReply Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

AutoReply Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

We do not collect:

  • Your messages or message content
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Any other personal information

Notification Access Permission

The app requires notification access permission to:

  • Read incoming WhatsApp message notifications
  • Send automatic replies using WhatsApp's notification reply system

All message processing happens locally on your device. No messages are sent to our servers or any third parties.

Data Storage

All data created by the app is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system:

  • Custom auto-reply rules
  • Default reply messages
  • App settings and preferences
  • Reply history logs

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

AutoReply Pro does not use:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection services

WhatsApp Integration

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to WhatsApp Inc. or Meta Platforms, Inc.

AutoReply Pro uses Android's standard notification system to interact with WhatsApp notifications. We do not access WhatsApp's internal data or databases.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device encryption and screen lock
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for adults managing professional or personal communications.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored on your device
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You can clear reply history within the app
  • You can revoke notification access permission at any time

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
